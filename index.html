Add bikes on saadl.com

This is the main feature of the website. Users join saadl to add photos of their bikes. It needs to be simple.

Add bikes buttons

They are located on top of the 2 bike feeds

If you are not logged, they toggle a popup to signup

Upload photos in a dropzone

Buttons toggle a popup in fullscreen.

Dropping your files anywhere instantly load your photos on the server

And redirect to your bike page listing all the photos you have uploaded

Identify the parts on your photos

Thousands of unique parts are registered in the database

A dropdown helps to select your parts by suggesting the most popular ones

Few parts later.. your bike page is set up

Global view

Let's first have a quick look at the entire structure

There are 2 types of blog posts: short posts and regular posts

Short posts

They are easy to write, quick to read and more frequent
For these reasons they have smaller thumbnails but are positioned on top

Hovering a thumbnail indicates the type of content: video or photos

Example of a short post

Regular posts

They are harder to write, longer to read and less frequent
For these reasons they have big thumbnails and are positioned below

Example of a regular post

At the end of each post, we highlight the credits, as a result users submit their own story to be published

Big sharing buttons make users share our stories

To keep the visitors, we end each post by the previous post of the same category
